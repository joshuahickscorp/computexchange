# render/ref/rack/D-ARCH.md · archetype decision · SIGNED OFF

Date: 2026-07-04. Grader ruling: "whichever one you think you can get to be the most
accurate in reproduction," refined same day: "something realistic that a person could own
... not some sort of server rack Elon's datacenter has ... could be big but still backyard
style representation." Dash gate: middot only.

DECISION: **A-prosumer · a populated standard 19in rack a PERSON could realistically own** ·
big is allowed (42U-class), but the fill is homelab/garage-miner grade, not hyperscaler:
a few 4U air-cooled GPU nodes (PCIe GPU chassis · the garage-AI-rig class, not DGX), one 1U
switch, a rackmount UPS at the bottom, zero-U PDU, cable management, blanking panels, and
some honestly EMPTY Us (no real person's rack is full). This variant strengthens both rack
tells: the fill is heterogeneous by nature (repetition variance comes free) and the
archetype is the one the site's viewer could actually BE · the modern equivalent of the
backyard BTC miner, earning uptime on owned hardware.

Under the grader's criterion the pick is unambiguous. A's geometry is OVER-DETERMINED:
the EIA-310 U-module is an exact standard (primary-source verified · 1U 44.45mm, holes
6.35/22.25/38.1mm from the U boundary, 9.5mm square cage-nut holes), the enclosure has
THREE independent dimensioned-CAD sources (Vertiv VR submittal drawings · APC NetShelter SX
install manual · Tripp Lite SmartRack submittal drawings), the node has exact vendor
mechanical specs (NVIDIA DGX B200 · 10U · 444 x 482.3 x 897.1mm), the switch face is fully
documented (QM9700 · 1U · 438 x 43.6mm · 2x16 OSFP cage grid), and part-level photography
exists at 1000-1500px on white backgrounds for tone/material pinning. B (GB200/GB300 NVL72)
fails the same criterion: no public mechanical drawings, real photography tops out
~800-1200px (~0.5px/mm at rack scale) under mixed show-floor light (unusable as tone pins
under the one-clean-source rule), NVIDIA first-party imagery is CGI · every dimension would
be triangulated, none anchored. C is a prop, not an archetype.
